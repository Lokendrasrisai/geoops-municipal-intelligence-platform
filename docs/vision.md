# GeoOps Municipal Intelligence Platform

## Vision

GeoOps is an ArcGIS-integrated municipal intelligence platform designed to help GIS consulting firms, municipalities, and utility operators understand, improve, and operationalize their GIS assets.

Rather than replacing ArcGIS, GeoOps augments existing GIS workflows through automated quality assessment, readiness scoring, operational intelligence, and decision-support capabilities.

The platform enables teams to move from raw spatial data toward actionable operational insight.

## Mission

Help organizations answer:

* What is wrong with my GIS data?
* What should I fix first?
* How ready am I for Utility Network migration?
* Which assets require immediate attention?
* What operational risks exist in my infrastructure network?

## Long-Term Goal

Become the operational intelligence layer sitting above ArcGIS workflows.
